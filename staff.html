Parin Dhami

Position: Photography Instructor

About Parin:

Parin Dhami is a passionate and experienced photography instructor who has been sharing his love for photography for over a decade. With a deep understanding of both the art and technical aspects of photography, he brings a unique perspective to his students.

Parin Dhami holds a Bachelor's degree in Fine Arts with a specialization in Photography. He has worked as a professional photographer, capturing a wide range of subjects, from breathtaking landscapes to captivating portraits. His diverse portfolio reflects his versatility and expertise in the field.

As an instructor, Parin's teaching approach is hands-on, interactive, and tailored to individual skill levels. He believes in the power of practice, experimentation, and continuous learning. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced photographer, Parin is dedicated to helping you enhance your skills and discover your unique creative voice.

Outside of the classroom, Parin continues to pursue his personal photography projects and is an active member of photography clubs and exhibitions. He's always excited to share his latest insights and experiences with his students.
